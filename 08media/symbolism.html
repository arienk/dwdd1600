Symbolism:

Symbolism is a movement throughout art and literature. Artworks made connections to topics like fears, desires, and impulses. There was a fascination with the dark recesses of the mind and this showed in art. Symbolsists rejected rationalism and instead explored emotions, imagination, and spirituality.

Elements-

  1. Focus on emotions
  2. Cryptic allusions
  3. The sublime
  4. Symbols
  5. Emphasis on the meaning being the acutal forms, lines, shapes, and colors in the art
  6. Dream-like
  7. Metaphorical
