Post-Impressionism:

Post-Impressionism is not a single style. Even though artists from the time don’t all have the same style they did draw from characteristics of Impressionism. Artists added onto Impressionism elements but added usually took one of two directions: a more analytical approach or a more expressive approach.

Elements-

  1. Many different styles
  2. Characteristics drawn from impressionism
  3. Structure vs. expression
  4. Expressive
  5. Color
