Romanticism:

Romanticism is an artistic movement that influenced more than just painting and architecture, it also influenced literature, music, and many other things. Romanticism rejects elements like balance and idealization, instead it goes towards the individaulization and more personal, the imaginative, and the more emotional. It originated in Europe peaking from 1790-1850. There was a celebration of nature and the common people. This period also brought along the idealization of women. Romanticism architecture was known as a gothic revival with eccentric elements.

Romanticists were more bound by goals and opinions instead of a strong specific style. During this period it was also common for artists to incorporate messages of many different topics including social justice and equality.

Elements-

  1. En plein air (painting outdoors)
  2. Appreciation of nature & landscapes
  3. Emotion
  4. Individualism
  5. Tension & darkness
  6. Dramatic compositions
  7. Dramatic lighting
  8. Viewer involvement
  9. Sublime: Feeling that is simultaneously inspiration and terrifying.
