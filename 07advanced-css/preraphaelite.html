Pre-Raphaelite:

Pre-Raphaelite artists were on a quest to bring back art from before the industrialization of the 1800s. They rejected the ideals of art that came from the British Royal Academy and made an alternative inside the artistic community. There was a revival of Renaissance and Medieval styles and stories. In 1848 the Pre-Raphaelite Brotherhood was founded and the style went on into the 1900s.

Elements-

  1. Bright colors
  2. Vivid detail
  3. Crisp lines and brushstrokes
  4. Naturalism
  5. Reminiscent of past art periods: Romanticism and Medieval art

Popular Artists from the Time-

  • John Everett Millais
  • Dante Gabriel Rossetti
  • William Holman Hunt
  • John William Waterhouse
  • Edward Burne-Jones
  • Evelyn De Morgan
  • William Morris
  • Emma Sandys
