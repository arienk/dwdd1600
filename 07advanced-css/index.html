During the 1800’s many artists began to rebel against the past art style and standards. There were rapid changes all around the world. The changes in art alone were incredibly dramatic. Artists were now creating works of their own without the guidance and restrictions from commissions. Perhaps this is why the art area had such a big change. With artists now doing the work they want to do, they were also exploring and experimenting. All of this led to a wide variety of movements, some short while others long and widespread.

Art Periods-

  • Romanticism: Peaked around 1790-1850.
  • Realism: Peaked around 1830-1880.
  • Pre-Raphaelite: Peaked around 1848-1900.
  • Impressionism: Peaked around 1870-1900.
  • Symbolism: Peaked around 1886-1900.
  • Post-Impressioinism: Peaked around 1886-1905.
  • Expressionism: Peaked around 1890-1939.
  • Art Nouveu: Peaked around 1890-1915.
