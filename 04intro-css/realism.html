Realism:

Realism is an artistic movement that was sparked by the Industrial Revolution. In this period, as the name says, art was made to show the real world around. Scenes were realistic, featuring everyday situations of “regular” people. It began in France and peaked around 1830-1870. Artists worked to make pieces as realistic as possible and with unidealized views of all of society.

Elements-

  1. A focus on everyday people and their settings/situations
  2. Rejection of romantic ideas
  3. Photorealistic
  4. Use of incredible detail
  5. Use of color

Popular Artists of the Time-

  • Elizabeth Nourse
  • Édouard Manet
  • Thomas Eakins
  • Gustave Courbet
  • Rosa Bonheur
  • Jean-Françios Millet
  • Honoré Daumier
